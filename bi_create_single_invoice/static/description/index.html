Single invoice from Multiple Sales and Purchase Orders Odoo Apps

If you are Odoo Accounting Users than everyday you have to make invoices and send it to your customers and add supplier invoices/vendor bills which you are getting from your suppliers. This Odoo apps allow to generate single invoice for multiple/many sales Order and purchase orders in Odoo ERP.

It's normal you , Get tired of making invoices from the sales and purchase by opening each records and click create invoice. And someone says "Want to generate just one invoice from many orders(PO,SO) ? Just by clicking on single button/actions . This Odoo apps add feature to generate single customer invoice or vendor bills from the multiple sales order and Purchase Orders in Odoo, you can make single invoice out of them with pretty easy steps. Just select multiple orders and go on action menu and click create invoice option and it's done. It reduce your lots of daily works and save your time.

Features

Invoice from sale/purchase order

This plugins helps you to make invoice from Orders whether its Sales or Purchase Orders it works for both.

Single invoice from multiple purchase order

This plugins helps you to create single invoice from multiple Purchase orders.

Single invoice from multiple sale order

This plugins helps you to create single invoice from multiple Sales Order.

Warning on selecting order not having same customer

Warning will be shown on selecting the order having different customer or if its invoice is already generated.
